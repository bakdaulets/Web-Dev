Recipe #1: Pilaf

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Butter or margarine 6 tbsp
Long grain rice 2 cups

Steps: Melt butter in a large pan, cook garlic and onions, add rice and broth, and simmer for 30 minutes. Add carrots and serve.

Recipe #2: Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Milk 1.5 cups

Steps: Mix flour, milk, and eggs into a smooth batter. Heat a non-stick pan and cook each pancake for 2 minutes per side. Serve with honey or jam.

Recipe #3: Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce 1 head
Cherry tomatoes 1 cup

Steps: Wash and chop the lettuce. Slice the cherry tomatoes. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
